Strict Warning

• Please read all instructions carefully before proceeding with your consultancy clearance.

As an individual candidate for a job replacement exercise it is expected that you should submit your clearance only once from the email address sent to you for a permanent and pensionable appointment at this agency!

How to Complete Your Application

  1. Ensure you have a valid National Identification Number (NIN)
  2. Gather all required documents for a documentation purpose (see list below)
  3. Fill in all required fields accurately
  4. Review your information before submission
  5. Submit successfully

The Required Document

NIN Slip

Eligibility Criteria

  • Nigerian citizen by birth
  • Age range: 18 to 35 years
  • Minimum height: 1.65m for males, 1.60m for females
  • Physically and mentally fit
  • No criminal record
